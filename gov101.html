The Executive

"Ang taga-luto ng Adobo."

They are the ones actually in the kitchen. They make sure the meat is bought and the stove is on. If the adobo is salty, everyone blames them.

🗣️

The Legislative

"Ang mga Ma-reklamo sa GC."

The group chat that never ends. They debate if the adobo should have potatoes or pineapples. They make the rules, but don't hold the ladle.

⚖️

The Judicial

"Ang Referee / 'The Adult'."

The one who tastes the adobo and checks if the GC rules were followed. If someone put pineapples against the rules, they call it out.
